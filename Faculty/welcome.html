Attendence

Enter the attendance of the students based on the class they are in.There is also the provision to edit the attendance of a whole class or student individually.

Marks

Enter the attendance of the students based on the class they are in.This includes Internals,events .The marks of the students can also be edited.

Timetable

View the timetable in a tabular form. For a particular class,it specifies a list of teachers who handle the same class that are free for that time slot.

Report

Generate reports for each class.These reports include generating a table consisting of the students belonging to that class and their respective Internal Marks and Attendence.
